WELCOME TO THE LEAN PHYSIQUE PROGRAM

This program is designed to help you achieve a muscular lean physique through a combination of tailored workouts and nutrition plans.

Day 1: Chest & Shoulders

    CHEST

  • Flat Bench Press: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Incline Bench Press: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Cable Chest Flies/Pec Dec Flies: 4 sets of 10-12 reps

    • SHOULDERS

  • Lateral Raises: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Reverse Pec Deck Flies: 4 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 2: Back & Triceps

    BACK

  • Lat PullDown: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Barbell Rows/TBar: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Standing Lat Pulldown: 4 sets of 15-20 reps

    • TRICEPS

  • Cable Tricep PushDowns: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Single Cross Body Tricep Cable PushDowns: 4 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 3: Legs & Biceps

    Legs

  • Squats/HackSquat: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Leg Extensions: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Leg Curls: 4 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Standing Calve Raises: 4 sets of 15-20 reps

    • BICEPS

  • Wide Barbell Curls: 3 sets of 10-12
  • Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Reverse Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 4: Rest and Recovery

Focus on stretching and light cardio to aid recovery.
