WELCOME TO THE SHREDDED PHYSIQUE PROGRAM

This program is designed to help you maintain your aesthetic physique through a combination of tailored workouts and nutrition plans.

Day 1: Shoulders and Arms

    SHOULDERS

  • Lateral Raises: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Reverse Pec Deck Flies: 3 sets of 6-8 reps

    • TRICEPS

  • Close Grip Bench Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Cable Over Head Tricep Extensions: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Single Arm Tricep Cable PushDowns: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

    • BICEPS

  • Wide Dumbell Curls: 3 sets of 10-12
  • Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Reverse Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 2: Legs

    LEGS

  • Squats/HackSquat: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Leg Extensions: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Leg Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Standing Weighted Calve Raises: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 3: Chest & Triceps

    CHEST

  • Incline Bench Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Flat Bench Press: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Weighted Dips: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Cable Chest Flies/Pec Dec Flies: 3 sets of 6-8 reps

    • TRICEPS

  • Cable Over Head Tricep Extensions: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Single Arm Tricep Cable PushDowns: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 4: Back and Biceps

    BACK

  • Weighted Pullups/Lat PullDown: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • TBar Rows/Barbell Rows: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • No Weight HyperExtensions: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Standing Lat Pulldown: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

    • BICEPS

  • Wide Dumbell Curls: 3 sets of 10-12
  • Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Reverse Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 5: Rest and Recovery

Focus on stretching and light cardio to aid recovery.
