WELCOME TO THE BULKY PHYSIQUE PROGRAM

This program is designed to help you achieve a muscular and strong physique through a combination of tailored workouts and nutrition plans.

Day 1: Chest & Shoulders

  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Pull-Ups: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Overhead Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 2:

  • Squats: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Day 3: Rest and Recovery

Focus on stretching and light cardio to aid recovery.
